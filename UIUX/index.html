Design Products People Love to Use

Course Structure

Your Journey Through the Design Process

Understand the core principles of user-centered design, learn the Design Thinking framework, and define user problems.

Conduct user interviews, create personas and journey maps, and build a solid information architecture.

Translate your research into low-fidelity wireframes and build interactive, clickable prototypes to test user flows.

Master color theory, typography, and layout principles. Learn to create design systems and apply branding to your interfaces.

Plan and conduct usability tests, analyze feedback for iteration, and prepare your final designs for developer handoff.

Apply the entire design process to a real-world project, culminating in a portfolio-ready case study to showcase your skills.
