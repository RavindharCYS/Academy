Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about career transition

Career Transition

How long does the transition typically take? +

The transition typically takes 6-12 months, depending on:

  • Your current skill set
  • Learning pace
  • Time commitment
  • Previous experience
Do I need a technical background? +

No, technical background is not mandatory. However, having analytical skills and willingness to learn technical tools is important. Many successful Business Analysts come from non-technical backgrounds.

Skills & Certifications

Which certification should I pursue first? +

For beginners, we recommend:

  • ECBA (Entry Certificate in Business Analysis)
  • CBAP (for experienced professionals)
  • Agile certifications
What are the essential skills I need to develop? +

Key skills include:

  • Requirements analysis
  • Business process modeling
  • Data analysis
  • Communication skills
  • Problem-solving abilities
